# Jam presentation – Snake 1v1 Arcade

## One-line pitch (for judges)

**"Competitive 1v1 snake with power-ups and neon arcade vibes – same screen, best of 3, instant fun."**

## First 30 seconds

1. **Title screen**: Shows "SNAKE 1v1 ARCADE" and controls (P1 WASD, P2 Arrows). Press **Enter** to start.
2. **Countdown**: 3 – 2 – 1 – GO (inputs locked until GO).
3. **Gameplay**: Two snakes (cyan vs magenta), one food pellet, power-ups spawn on the grid. Clear HUD with round score and "MATCH POINT" when 2–1 or 1–2.

## Presentation flow for judges

- **Sensación Arcade**: No tutorial needed; controls on screen; responsive grid movement.
- **Rejugabilidad**: "Play again" after match; power-ups and food placement vary each round.
- **Claridad**: Distinct colors, immediate SFX on collect/collision, obvious round winner.
- **Pulido**: Full loop (menu → game → results → play again); no placeholders.
- **Creatividad**: 1v1 twist + speed/ghost/growth power-ups.
